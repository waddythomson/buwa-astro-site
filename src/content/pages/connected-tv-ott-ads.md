## Common Questions Asked About CTV | OTT

### What is the difference between CTV and OTT?

Connected TV (CTV) refers to devices like Smart TVs and gaming consoles that are directly linked to the internet such as Apple TV or Roku. On the other hand, Over-The-Top (OTT) refers to content via the Internet, bypassing traditional cable boxes. OTT ads play on popular streaming platforms like ESPN, CNN, and FOX.

### How long is the CTV | OTT ad?

When creating the CTV | OTT ad, you will be able to choose between a 15-second ad or a 30-second ad.
