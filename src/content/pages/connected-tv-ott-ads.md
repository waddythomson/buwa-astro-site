Connected TV (CTV) and Over-The-Top (OTT) advertising delivers your message directly to viewers streaming content on smart TVs, gaming consoles, and streaming devices. Unlike traditional TV, CTV/OTT offers precise targeting and measurable results.

## Why Connected TV Advertising?

### Reach Cord-Cutters

Millions of viewers have moved from traditional cable to streaming services. CTV/OTT advertising ensures your brand reaches these audiences where they're actually watching.

### Non-Skippable Format

Unlike YouTube or social media ads, CTV ads are typically non-skippable, ensuring viewers see your complete message.

### Premium Content Environment

Your ads appear alongside premium content from trusted networks, enhancing brand credibility and viewer engagement.

### Precise Targeting

Target viewers based on demographics, interests, viewing habits, and geographic location—including specific areas around Columbia, SC.
