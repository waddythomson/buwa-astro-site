## Why Digital Menu Boards?

### Increase Sales

Dynamic visuals and strategic placement of high-margin items can increase average check sizes. Studies show digital menus can boost sales by 3-5%.

### Easy Updates

Change prices, add seasonal items, or promote daily specials instantly—no printing costs or wait times.

### Enhanced Customer Experience

Bright, clear displays make ordering easier and reduce perceived wait times. Show off your food with appetizing photos and videos.

### Dayparting

Automatically switch between breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus at scheduled times.
