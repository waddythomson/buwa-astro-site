## Pre-Roll Advertising

Create a memorable brand experience with engaging online video content.

[Get In Touch](/contact-us)

**Pre-Roll ads are short videos that appear before online videos. Lasting 15 to 30 seconds, these non-skippable ads effectively tell your unique brand story. Generate powerful brand awareness with Pre-Roll Video Ads.**

## Pre-Roll Ads can Increase Your Brand Awareness and Traffic

Pre-Roll advertising gives you the ability to stand out in the increasingly competitive digital landscape. With the explosive growth of online video consumption, Pre-Roll ads provide direct access to highly targeted audiences. These visually captivating ads are a great way to drive brand awareness and are a powerful addition to [Google Business Profile](/google-business-profile) strategies, [Social Media Advertising](/social-media-ads), or [CTV | OTT](/connected-tv-ott-ads) campaigns.

## Benefits of Pre-Roll Advertising

### Powerful Targeting

Pre-Roll video ads can be targeted to viewers' habits, demographics, and interests. Through options of online behavior, interests, location, and keywords, we can precisely target the ideal audience. This level of precision ensures that our ads reach the right people, maximizing the impact of our marketing efforts.

![Pre-Roll Ads - Powerful Targeting](/uploads/2024/04/Effective-Targeting.jpg)

### Guaranteed Views

Our non-skippable Pre-Roll ads appear at the beginning of online video content, ensuring your story is more likely to be seen than ads that appear in the middle or at the end of a video.

![Pre-Roll Ads - Guaranteed Views](/uploads/2024/04/Guarantee-Views.jpg)

### Improve Brand Awareness

Incorporating Pre-Roll ads into your marketing plan helps reinforce your business's values and message, boosting customer confidence and increasing brand recognition among your target audience.

### Captivating Visuals

Pre-Roll ads gives you the ability to capture your viewers' attention at the most crucial moment of their online journey. With stunning and fully immersive visuals, you can be confident that your ads are reaching—and impacting—the audiences that matter.

## Common Questions About Pre-Roll

### How long are pre-roll ads?

All pre-roll ads are either 15 or 30 seconds. [Let us help you](/contact-us) determine which duration is the right fit for you.

### How effective are pre-roll ads?

Pre-roll videos are one of the most effective forms of digital advertising. We strive to optimize the performance of each Pre-Roll campaign to ensure maximum ROI targeting strategy and how interested viewers are in the product or service being offered.

### Can I use Pre-Roll and CTV | OTT advertising together?

Yes! A multi-channel campaign that covers Pre-Roll and OTT advertising will help maximize ROI on all digital advertising platforms. When in conjunction with foundation regeneration marketing, these campaigns are most effective alongside foundational and lead-generating marketing.
