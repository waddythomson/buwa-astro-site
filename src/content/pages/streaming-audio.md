## Streaming Audio Common Questions

### What is involved with creative production?

After conducting a thorough consultation to understand your goals and messaging, we handle all aspects of creative production for you. Once we've gathered your input, we'll begin crafting your audio ad and you'll have the opportunity to review and approve the final output before it's submitted to the chosen platforms.

### How effective is it?

Audio advertising can be highly effective in reaching and engaging audiences, especially when targeted correctly with a compelling message. Its ability to create emotional connections and deliver messages in a non-intrusive way can lead to increased brand awareness and engagement.

### How is a Streaming ad different than my local radio station?

Streaming Audio Advertising differs from local radio ads in its automation, data-driven targeting, and real-time optimization capabilities. Unlike traditional radio ads, Streaming audio allows for precise audience segmentation, and dynamic ad content delivery regardless of the time of day, resulting in more efficient and effective campaigns tailored to specific audience segments and objectives.
