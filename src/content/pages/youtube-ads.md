## YouTube Ad Formats

### Skippable In-Stream Ads

Play before, during, or after videos. Viewers can skip after 5 seconds, and you only pay when they watch 30 seconds or engage.

### Non-Skippable In-Stream Ads

15-second ads that play in full, ensuring your complete message is seen.

### Bumper Ads

6-second non-skippable ads perfect for quick, memorable brand impressions.

### Discovery Ads

Appear in YouTube search results and alongside related videos, reaching viewers actively browsing.
