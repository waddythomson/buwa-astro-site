## 4 Main Reasons Businesses Hire Us

### Lack of Resources

Anything from manpower, budget, and time, needed for effective social media management. With competing priorities like operations and customer service, social media may not receive the necessary attention, hindering consistent content creation and audience engagement. As a result, businesses may struggle to keep pace with the ever-evolving social media landscape and may miss out on key trends and changes.

### Lack of Expertise

Without specialized skills in content creation, audience targeting, and analytics, businesses may struggle to develop and implement effective strategies. This can lead to inconsistent messaging and ineffective use of social media platforms.

### Time Constraints

This is a significant challenge for small business owners who are often juggling multiple responsibilities. Regular posting, monitoring, and engagement with followers require dedicated time, which may be in short supply. Consequently, posts may be infrequent, responses to messages delayed, and opportunities for audience engagement missed.

### Keeping Up With Trends

Social media can be daunting for small business owners. The emergence of new platforms, features, and trends requires continuous learning and adaptation. However, the time and effort required to stay updated may be prohibitive for businesses already stretched thin. As a result, businesses may struggle to adapt their social media strategy effectively, potentially falling behind competitors.

## Common Questions About Social Media Management

### How much does Social Media Management cost?

The cost can vary according to a businesses needs, goals and the overall scope of the project. Smaller businesses that are just starting out it is best to recommend a starter pack. Those that wish to take their social media to another level then we recommend packages that include [paid advertising](/social-media-ads).

### What if I want to continue making posts on my own?

Sure you can, many owners do. All we ask is that we agree on your schedule and types of content that you may be posting so we do not overlap or contradict any messaging.

### Do I need to give you my password?

As long as you are an administrator on your account then you can allow us as an agency to manage your accounts. We will walk you through this process during our discovery phase.
